Competitor Comments
BLUETILE SKATESHOP Advantages: The site has a very clean layout. Aside from the products they offer, there is a link to their blog.
Disadvantages: The logo does not direct the user back to the homepage, and there is no "home" hyperlink. Some of the products offered on the website does not have a provided picture or product description. The "news" hyperlink offers no information.
INLAND SURF & SKATE Advantages: The site is easy to navigate and the layout is appealingly simple. They provide an embedded google map with directions, making it quick and easy for a customer to locate the establishment.
Disadvantages: Very little information is provided about the store. Instead of pictures, they provide videos of some of the products they offer. The photo gallery doesn't showcase the products they offer or information on the merchandise.
CONTINUUM SKATESHOP Advantages: The site is easy to navigate and includes a search bar tool. The product descriptions are very detailed. There is a zoom feature on the product pictures, to get a closer look.
Disadvantages: The home page layout looks more like a blog and is simply boring. They don't offer a lot of product variety.
TACTICS SKATESHOP Advantages: The site includes a search bar tool, and is easy to navigate. You can search by brand, and they offer a lot of product variety. Their "sale" section provides information on the percentage the product has been discounted by.
Disadvantages: The text is very small. Although the color scheme is appealing, the website information looks cluttered.
A1 SKATEBOARDS SKATEBOARD SHOP Advantages: The offer many options to find exactly what you're looking for, narrowing search results down by brand, color, and by pro skateboarder products. They offer live online support to assist customers with questions or concerns, and also provide a "track my order" feature to check the status of your purchase.
Disadvantages: The "About Us" section is not listed on the home page and can only be accessed after following multiple links. The layout seems unorganized and littered with various advertisements at first glance, although these are really links.

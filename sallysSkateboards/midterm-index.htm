About Us

 

Voted #1 in the Lowcountry by Thrasher Skateboard Magazine three years in a row, Sally's Skateboards is your one stop shop for all of your skateboard needs. Sally's Skateboards offer a wide variety of your favorite skateboard brands to choose from. Do you need a new skateboard? Are you in need of some skateboard accessories, or tools and supplies to maintain your skateboard? We've got you covered! We are not currently selling our merchandise online, but you can visit our North Charleston location at 205 Jolly Road. Below is a list of some of the complete skateboard decks and wheels that we offer. Only at Sally's Skateboards can you get the inside scoop on how to build your own skateboard ramps, and perform jumps. For additional information or questions about Sally's Skateboards, please contact us at sallysk8@gmail.com, or give us a call at (843) 221-5766.
